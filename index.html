About Me

Hello! My name is Florencio A. Duran and I am currently an undergraduate student at IIT in Electrical Engineering. My hope is to someday go into power electronics at a high tech company where I can apply what I've learned from circuit analysis, electrodynamics, and signals and systems. I will be graduating from IIT in December of 2019 with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering. I received an A.S. in Science from Harold Washington College in December of 2016. My interests span multiple interdisciplinary interests from robotics, circuits, design, autonomy, AI, digital electronics, controls, and music.
